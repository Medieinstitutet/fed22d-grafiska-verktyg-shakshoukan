About the film

Welcome to Mars follows a team of German astronauts who are sent on a mission to become the first humans to land on Mars. As they land, they quickly realize that they are not alone. Follow along on the journey through the depths of space where they encounter a host of challenges, including dangerous asteroids, malfunctioning equipment, and a mysterious virus that threatens to wipe out the crew. With the help of their wits and advanced technology, the team must overcome these obstacles and emerge victorious, paving the way for future generations to explore the red planet. But as they draw closer to their destination, they begin to uncover secrets about the true purpose of their mission, leading them to question whether they can trust their superiors and each other.
